Chicken Parmesan

TAGS: Italian, Chicken, Tomato, Dairy



Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to season
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsely
  • 3 large chicken breasts, halved
  • 1 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 14 oz. tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon Italian herbs

Recipe

  • 1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a medium sized pot.
  • 2. Fry onions until transparent.
  • 3. Add garlic and cook for additional 30 seconds
  • 4. Add tomato puree, salt, pepper, Italian herbs.
  • 5. Simmer for about 8 minutes
  • 6. Preheat oven to 430F|220C.
  • 7. Lightly grease oven tray with non stick cooking spray
  • 8. Whisk together eggs, garlic, parsely, salt, and pepper
  • 9. Add chicken to the egg mixture, evenly coating each filet.
  • 10. Allow to marinate for at least 15 minutes
  • 11. Place chicken on the sprayed oil tray with1/3 cup of sauce.
  • 12. Top each chicken breast with mozzarella and parmesan cheese and parsely
  • 13. Bake for 15-20 minutes until cheese is melted and chicken is cooked through

Common Variations and Substitutes

  • Substitute 1/2 cup italian breadcrumbs for 1/2 cup of panko
  • Substitute equal amount of onion powder for garlic powder
